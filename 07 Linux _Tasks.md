## Task 61: Disk Space Monitoring and Cleanup Strategy

### Objective
Prevent disk space issues and recover storage.

### Requirements
- Create large files to simulate disk usage  
- Analyze disk space utilization  
- Identify directories consuming the most space  
- Remove unnecessary files  
- Verify available space has increased
